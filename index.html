Learning is good

Learning is something that takes place daily by interacting with other people and the world around us. Life often throws challenges at us, there may be hurdles to overcome, sometimes we compare ourselves with others who perform better than us, but one should never get demotivated.
We now accept the fact that learning is a lifelong process of keeping abreast of change. And the to teach people how to learn.
Peter Drucker

Life is long

Lifelong learning is possible for a curious learner only. Each passing day has something new for us and we hope these lifelong learning quotes help you in your growth.
